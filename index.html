Welcome to the Wonderous World of Chickpeas

This wonderous food has been in our diet for roughly 7,500 years. There is a reason they are still so popular to this day.

Science Backed Facts

  • First culitivated in the Middle East 7,500 years BC
  • Other names include garbanzo beans, bengal grams, Egyptian peas, ceci beans and kabuli chana
  • These plants actually restore depleted soils and are powerful nitrogen fixing legumes
  • Ground Chickpeas have been used as a coffee substitue since the 18th century and ar estill commnoly used as a caffeine-free alternative today
  • Chickpeas come in a variety of different types and color, such as black, green, red and brown
